Welcome! The app I decided to create is an inspiration of the classic game snake. To play the game press start. Then use the arrow keys to control the snake UP arrow to make the snake go up DOWN to go down LEFT to got left and RIGHT to go right. The objective in this game to collect 50 points to collect points get the snake to eat the apple each time you eat the apple you gain 5 points and the snakes tail will grown. Beware of the walls if you make contact with the wall you will be sent back to the start screen.
